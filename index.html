EDITH EJOH MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIP

The Edith Ejoh Memorial SCholarship offers renewable scholarships to female students with financial needs who display academic achievement, a commitment to serving others (in school, their community, or at home), a strong work ethic, and leadership qualities.

Our Mission

Helping Agulerian Female Students achieve their dreams

Female students face a variety of challenges when pursuing higher education. From being unable to afford tuition, to not having access to resources that can provide guidance and support, these challenges have prevented many females from getting their degrees. This scholarship seeks to change this by providing funding for female students as they pursue their educational goals

Providing financial assistance to female students of Aguleri origin in any Nigerian University who want to study EDUCATION or MEDICINE or already studying the above disciplines in the University
