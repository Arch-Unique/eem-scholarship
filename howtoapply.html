PREAMBLE

Dr. EDITH CHINEZE EJOH MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIP was established In December 2020 by some friends of late Dr. EDITH CHINEZE EJOH to immortalise her legacies. The scholarship is meant to provide financial assistance to suitable qualified female student of Aguleri origin in any Nigerian University who want to study EDUCATION or MEDICINE or already studying the above disciplines in the University. The value of the scholarship is Two Hundred Thousand (#200.000.00) Naira only per annum for a successful female candidate to be selected strictly on merit.

To assist with the collection and processing of Application forms, applicants are to submit completed forms to the below email:

Email: eem.scholarship@gmail.com

REQUIREMENTS

- Evidence of a good WAEC or GCE or NECO result

- JAMB Letter of Admission or Letter of evidence of already studying in a University

- Passport Photograph

- Identification Letter from the traditional Ruler of Aguleri evidencing that the applicant is from Aguleri.
